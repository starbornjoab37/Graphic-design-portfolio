Hi, I’m Ombam Judith Beril Atieno

Welcome! I design from scratch to solve communication challenges in classrooms, hostels, and communities. Every project is created with intention, clarity, and warmth.

Selected Work

All projects started as real problems that needed creative solutions. From confusion to disengagement, my designs transform challenges into visual communication people can feel, understand, and act on.

Success Card for My Sister

I created a personal success card for my sister that everyone in her class loved. The design was crafted entirely from scratch, focusing on warmth, encouragement, and memorable recognition.

Hostel Theft Awareness Poster

I designed a poster to address the ongoing theft of clothes in my hostel. The goal was to raise awareness and communicate urgency without blame or panic. Entirely created from scratch.

Village-Based Meetup Poster

I developed a poster for a village-based meetup group. Most communication was text-based, so I created something clear, inviting, and engaging from scratch. The Nairobi version shows several iterations to improve clarity and impact.

Several iterations were required to achieve clarity and simplicity. The final design balances spacing, visual importance, and approachability.
